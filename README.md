# Hub 2: Enterprise Language Services

**Advocate Health | AVP Emmanuel's Language Services Function**

The most comprehensive hub of the four, covering interpretation operations, census/migration intelligence, regulatory tracking, vendor performance, modality routing logic, failure case studies, and connect-to-purpose stories across a 6-state, 247-language, $42.8M operation.

## Sections (14 navigable, all drillable)

- **Dashboard** -- 4 KPI drill-down cards with regional and vendor summaries
- **Operations** -- Languages with modality mix (in-person/VRI/OPI %), regions with full metrics
- **Census & Migration** -- LEP population trends, 5 fastest-growing languages, USCIS alerts
- **Laws & Regulation** -- 6 regulatory items with full details, impact, actions, deadlines
- **Vendor Dashboard** -- 5 vendors with 6 surface metrics + expandable detailed performance
- **Modality Routing** -- Interactive simulator with cost impact + 8 clinical setting rules
- **Cost Modeling** -- 3 scenarios (incl. AI-Augmented saving $3.8M), 8 expandable budget lines
- **Predictive Analytics** -- 3 AI models (Demand 91.8%, Vendor SLA 88.4%, Cost Optimization 82.1%)
- **Connect to Purpose** -- 4 patient/community stories with measurable impact metrics
- **Case Studies** -- 5 global failures with Advocate Health prevention analysis
- **Feedback Loops** -- 4 channels (patients, interpreters, clinicians, community)
- **Governance** -- 4 bodies including Interpreter Advisory Council
- **Data Aggregation** -- 6 connected sources with quality scores
- **Projects** -- 8 expandable projects including KIP-Coordinator Pro

## Capabilities Built In

- Structured data aggregation (6 sources: Epic, Interpreter CMS, Census/USCIS, Vendor APIs, Patient Satisfaction, Financial)
- Feedback loops (4 channels with findings and actions)
- Governance tracking (4 bodies with recent decisions)
- Predictive optimization (3 AI models with actionable predictions)
- Cost modeling (3 scenarios with budget drill-downs)
- Modality routing logic (8 clinical settings with recommended/fallback modalities + interactive cost simulator)
- AI Agent with smart routing for demand, vendor, modality, and cost queries
